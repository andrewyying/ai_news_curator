# Label Schema for News Classification

## Categories

### AI Models
News about new model releases, model capabilities, model comparisons, model updates, and model-related products.

**Examples:**
- "OpenAI releases GPT-5"
- "New language model achieves SOTA on benchmarks"
- "Comparison: GPT-4 vs Claude 3"

### AI Infrastructure & Hardware
News about GPUs, TPUs, cloud infrastructure, training infrastructure, hardware accelerators, and compute resources.

**Examples:**
- "NVIDIA announces new H100 GPU"
- "AWS launches new AI training instances"
- "New TPU architecture for large-scale training"

### AI Research
Academic papers, research breakthroughs, new techniques, theoretical advances, and research publications.

**Examples:**
- "New paper on transformer architecture improvements"
- "Researchers propose novel attention mechanism"
- "Breakthrough in few-shot learning"

### AI Policy & Regulation
Government policies, regulations, ethics guidelines, safety standards, and legal frameworks related to AI.

**Examples:**
- "EU passes AI Act"
- "New guidelines for AI safety"
- "Regulatory framework for AI deployment"

### Developer Tools & Platforms
APIs, SDKs, frameworks, developer-facing products, tools for building AI applications, and platforms for developers.

**Examples:**
- "New Python library for fine-tuning LLMs"
- "OpenAI releases new API features"
- "Framework for building RAG applications"

### Tech Business & Strategy
Company news, acquisitions, business strategies, market trends, funding rounds, and industry analysis.

**Examples:**
- "Microsoft acquires AI startup"
- "AI company raises $100M in funding"
- "Market analysis: AI industry growth"

### Other
Anything that doesn't fit the above categories, including general tech news, non-AI tech news, or unclear/ambiguous content.
